## Why this site exists

This website is designed as a lightweight personal operating system. It keeps different kinds of progress visible without forcing everything into a heavy database or admin panel.

## Current focus

- Build small software tools that solve real workflow problems.
- Turn research notes into structured essays.
- Track career momentum with enough context to make better decisions.

## Maintenance rhythm

Content lives in Markdown and data files, which keeps the system transparent. A new blog post is just a new file. A software update is just one project entry. The goal is low friction and high signal.
